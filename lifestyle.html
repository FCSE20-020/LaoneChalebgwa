LIFESTYLE EXPERIENCE IN BERLIN

1 .NIGHTLIFE IN BERLIN

Art galleries and live music venue are often open till late in the city and most of the tourists and locals who wish to enjoy such can visit .Clubs are also found open during the night for those who wish to have fun with loud music and beer.

2 .SHOPPING IN BERLIN

Shopping here is an experience defined by both style and originality .The Ku'damm seems a bit claustrophobic with both locals and tourists during the shopping season at the city.

3 .EATING OUT IN BERLIN

Most of the food in Berlin are found to be fuss-free and delicious. Most local cuisine are influenced by immigrants and neighbouring countries.The city also a host to a top quality resturants.

4 .OUTDOOR ACTIVITIES IN BERLIN

Football is the most popular sport in the capital but some activities such as horse riding and hiking are also taken into consideration. Parks found in the city gives cyclists and runners to enjoy the fresh air in abundance.
